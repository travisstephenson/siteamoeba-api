FAQ

Common questions

What kind of pages does this work with?

SiteAmoeba works with any web page — sales pages, landing pages, lead gen pages, e-commerce product pages, webinar registration pages, free trial pages, checkout pages. If it has a URL and you want more conversions, it works. Compatible with GoHighLevel, WordPress, Shopify, Webflow, Unbounce, ClickFunnels, Kartra, custom HTML, and anything else with a <head> tag.

How many tests can I run?

All plans include unlimited campaigns. Free tier runs 1 concurrent test at a time. Pro runs 3, Business runs 5, and Autopilot has no limit — run as many simultaneous tests as you want.

What does the tracking actually capture?

Everything. Scroll depth (25%, 50%, 75%, 100%), individual section visibility (which sections each visitor actually sees), every click with element identification, video play and completion events, time-on-page down to the second, and device type. All of this is captured automatically with a single script tag — no configuration needed.

What's BYOK?

Bring Your Own Key. Connect your own API keys from OpenAI (GPT-4), Anthropic (Claude), Google (Gemini), Mistral, xAI (Grok), or Meta (Llama). The free tier lets you use your own keys for unlimited testing at zero cost. Your tests still contribute to the Brain's learning (unless you're on Enterprise), and you still get full behavioral tracking and analytics.

What makes the Brain different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT generates generic copy. The Brain is purpose-built for conversion optimization. It's trained on real sales psychology — offer architecture, unique mechanisms, psychological triggers, proven headline frameworks, section-specific strategies. It knows which techniques work for which page types. And critically, it learns from every test running on the platform. When a variant wins anywhere in the system, that pattern gets incorporated. The more tests that run, the smarter your suggestions get.

What happens after beta?

Beta users lock in half-price for the lifetime of their subscription. When we move to full pricing, your rate stays the same as long as your subscription is active.

How does Autopilot work?

Autopilot monitors your running tests continuously. When a variant reaches statistical significance (typically 90%+ confidence), it automatically promotes the winner, retires losing variants, and generates new challengers. This creates an always-improving optimization loop — your page gets better every week without you touching it.
